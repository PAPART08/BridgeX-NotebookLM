Questions? Let's clear things up.

How does the free trial work?

You get full access to all Plus features for 7 days — no credit card required upfront. Poke around, try everything, see if it fits. If you like it, pick a plan. If not, no hard feelings.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Yep. No cancellation fees, no guilt trips, no sad emails. Just click cancel in your account settings and you're done. You'll keep Plus until your billing period ends.

What happens to my stuff if I cancel?

Your notebooks stay safe — we don't delete anything. But here's the deal: any folders, tags, or prompts you created beyond the free limits will be hidden until you resubscribe. Think of it like your extra stuff taking a little nap. It's all still there, waiting for you to come back.

(And yes, this means you can't just subscribe for a month, create 50 folders, and cancel. We see you. 👀)

Is the lifetime deal actually lifetime?

100%. One payment, and you're set forever. No renewals, no "oh wait, there's a catch." Just permanent access to Plus. We pinky promise.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit cards and bank transfers — both processed securely through Paystack. Quick, safe, and painless.
